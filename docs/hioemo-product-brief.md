# Hioemo Product Brief

## One-Liner
Hioemo is an AI-native short-video network on Solana where every like is programmable: fans can reward creators instantly, creators can reward fans back, and AI agents coordinate both sides.

## Core Formula
Hioemo = short-video social graph + programmable likes + AI-managed two-way rewards

## Core Insight
Traditional social platforms treat likes as vanity signals. Web3 tipping systems turn support into a heavy wallet flow. Hioemo turns the most common social action, the like, into a lightweight economic primitive.

This creates a new interaction loop:

- fan -> like -> creator gets paid
- creator -> reward back -> fan gets recognized / paid
- AI agents coordinate both sides

## Product Thesis
Every like is programmable.

That means a like is no longer only:

- a signal of taste
- a ranking input
- a passive engagement metric

It becomes:

- an instant micropayment
- a trigger for fan rewards
- a rule-driven social contract between fan and creator

## Why Solana
Hioemo only works if high-frequency interactions can settle cheaply and fast enough to feel native inside a content feed.

Solana is a strong fit because it enables:

- low-cost micropayments at social scale
- near-instant confirmation for like actions
- wallet-native user ownership
- programmable reward flows between fans and creators
- future composability with creator treasuries, referral loops, and sponsor incentives

## Who We Start With
Initial wedge: AI creators, micro-influencers, and independent video creators who want better monetization than ad-sharing and better fan retention than one-way tipping.

Expansion wedge: food, lifestyle, and local merchant creators who can turn community engagement into measurable customer acquisition.

## Main User Problem
Creators today have three broken options:

- likes generate no direct income
- tipping is too heavy for everyday interactions
- fan loyalty is difficult to identify and reward systematically

Fans also have no good system for lightweight, programmable support:

- subscriptions are too blunt
- tips are too infrequent
- loyalty is rarely recognized in a transparent way

## Hioemo Solution
Hioemo combines a short-video feed with programmable reward rails.

### Fan Side
- Set a daily like budget once
- Swipe videos and like naturally
- A like can send a micropayment such as `0.01 USDC`
- Earn creator-defined fan rewards for high-value engagement

### Creator Side
- Receive micropayments instantly when fans like content
- Define reward rules for fans
- Use an AI agent to identify valuable fans and trigger reward programs
- Grow a creator treasury instead of waiting for platform payouts

## AI Agents
The first version should focus on two agent layers.

### CAA: Creator AI Agent
The Creator AI Agent helps creators:

- generate titles, captions, and posting suggestions
- identify high-quality fans based on interaction patterns
- configure fan reward rules
- decide when to reward back top supporters
- summarize earnings and engagement performance

### FAA: Fan AI Agent
The Fan AI Agent helps fans:

- set and manage a daily support budget
- discover creators aligned with their interests
- optimize support across favorite creators
- track earned recognition, rewards, and status

## What Makes This New
Hioemo is not just a short-video app with tipping.

The novelty is the two-way programmable reward relationship:

- fans can support with one-tap micropayments
- creators can algorithmically reward fan behavior back
- both sides can rely on AI to manage behavior and incentives

This moves the product from "creator monetization" into "programmable social capital."

## MVP
The MVP should prove one story only:

Every like is programmable.

### MVP Features
1. Short-video swipe feed
2. Creator profiles
3. One-time daily support budget authorization
4. Like-triggered micropayment to creator
5. Creator dashboard for earnings and top supporters
6. Creator-defined fan reward campaign
7. CAA recommendations for top fan rewards
8. FAA budget and support assistant

## Example Loop
1. A fan sets a daily support budget of `$1.00`.
2. The fan likes a creator video.
3. The system sends `0.01 USDC` to the creator.
4. The Creator AI Agent identifies the fan as highly engaged.
5. The creator has a rule to reward the top 100 fans.
6. The fan receives `0.02 USDC`, recognition, or a special access badge.

## Why This Can Win
This concept is strong for a hackathon because it is:

- easy to understand in one sentence
- visibly crypto-native
- demonstrable in a short live flow
- commercially legible as a creator economy startup
- extendable into loyalty, commerce, sponsorship, and community finance

## What We Are Not Building First
To stay sharp, the first version should not center on:

- a full ad marketplace
- a broad token economy
- complex DeFi strategy UIs
- NFT-first social mechanics
- generic AI chat for everything

## Phase 2
Once the core loop is proven, Hioemo can expand into:

- sponsor-funded creator campaigns
- brand reward matching
- local business creator collaborations
- creator treasury auto-growth vaults
- gated fan clubs and premium social tiers

## Pitch Sentence
Hioemo turns likes from passive engagement into programmable value, creating a new AI-native social economy where fans and creators can reward each other directly on Solana.
